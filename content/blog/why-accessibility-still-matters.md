# What Do I Mean By Accessibility?

*Accessibility - The ability to access*

Related to web development, this means the users ability to access your website, app, resource, material, or data.
It is not only important for people with disabilities who may not be able to access a website like other users, but is also important for people with other accessability concerns for example; slow internet connections, or using a  mobile device, ...

The web was founded on interconnectivity, connecting people and resources together to form a 'Web' hence the terms 'World Wide Web' and 'Website'
